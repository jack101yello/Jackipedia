# Contractible Groupoid

A contractible groupoid is a [[Category]] which is equivalent to the terminal category $1$ under an [[Equivalence of Categories]].

## The Category of Elements

For any [[Set-Valued Functor]] $F$ on a category $C$, the full subcategory of the [[Category of Elements]] of $F$ spanned by its [[Representable Functor|representations]] is either empty or is a contractible groupoid.
