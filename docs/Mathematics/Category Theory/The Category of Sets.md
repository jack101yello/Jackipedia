# The Category of Sets

The category of sets, denoted $\text{Set}$, is the locally-small [[Category]] whose objects are all sets and whose morphisms are all functions between those sets. It is one of the most commonly-used categories in [[Category Theory]], especially within the context of [[The Yoneda Lemma]].

A [[Functor]] whose codomain is the category of sets is said to be set-valued. A [[Contravariant Functor]] which is set-valued is called a [[Pre-Sheaf]].
