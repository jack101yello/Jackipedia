# Sierpinski Space

The Sierpinski space $S$ is a [[Topological Space]] consisting of two points, $0$, and $1$, with [[Topology]] $\mathcal{T} = \{\emptyset, 1, S\}$.

## Category Theory

Both the [[Functor]] which sends a topological space to its set of [[Open Set]]s and that the functor which sends a topological space to its set of [[Closed Set]]s are [[Representable Functor]]s, and are in fact both represented by the Sierpinski space.
