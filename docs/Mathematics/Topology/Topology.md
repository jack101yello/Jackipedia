# Topology

Topology is a field of mathematics which studies sets imbued with a notion of neighborhood, but not necessarily with a metric. Generically, it is the study of [[Topological Space]]s.

## Of a Topological Space

A topological space $(X, \mathcal{T})$ consists of a set $X$ and a "topology" $\mathcal{T}$, which is a set of subsets of $X$ called the [[Open Set]]s of the topology.
